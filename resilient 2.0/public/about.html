What is Resilient India?

We would like to introduce ourselves as an NGO working in the field of Disaster Risk Reduction and Crime Prevention. To provide Comprehensive expertise and support for all Ex-ante and Ex-post Disaster Management and Mitigation activities , Training and Capacity Building to make the Country and our fellow citizens Disaster Resilient.

Our Mission

Saving Lives and Livelihoods

Promoting Strategies for Sustainable Management of Crime, Risks and Resources.

To reduce the long term impact of Natural as well as Manmade disasters and achieve sustainable growth.

To work with vulnerable communities to empower them to become resilient against crime and violence.

To work with communities by adopting a collaborative approach, sharing knowledge and experience.
