CURRENT PROJECTS

Projects We Are Working On.

1. Urban Crime and Violence Prevention Project with Nashik Police Commissionerate and IG Police, Nashik Range, Maharashtra

2. Implementation of School /College Safety Programme to prevent Youth Violence and Bullying under the School/College Urban Crime and Violence Prevention Programme

3. Ashoka Education Foundation : Running School Campus Safety Programme successfully in the Wadala and Chandshi Campuses to counter Bullying, Sexual Harassment , Cyber Crime ,Accidents etc.

4. Hazard, Vulnerability, Risk & Capacity Analysis of National Highways under the Jurisdiction of NHAI, M.S. with an aim to design interventions to bring down the numbers of casualties resulting from Road accidents on NH-3 Mumbai -Agra Highway & NH-50 Nashik-Pune Highway.

  • Imparting Training to NHAI personnel / Agencies /Concessionaire in First response for Emergency Management during accidents to minimize loss of lives and goods.

5. Entrusted with Crowd Management and Disaster Management for the Shree Saptashrungi Niwas Devi Trust, Nashik. The entire project is divided into four phases that includes Planning, Sourcing, Training, and Implementation

6. Imparting Training to the HSP (Highway Safety Police) Maharashtra personnel in First response for Emergency Management on State and National Highways.

7. Collaboration for implementation of Comprehensive Road Safety Measures Evaluation in the jurisdiction of RTO , Nashik and to strengthen the License issuing mechanism

8. Addl. Director General of Police (Traffic), Maharashtra : Training of Traffic Police Personnel and Highway Police Personnel in “High Risk Accident Response Procedures on Highways.’’

9. Addl. Director General of Police (Traffic), Maharashtra : Design and formulation of interventions and Modules to bring down Road Accident Fatalities in Nashik District as per the guidelines laid down by Hon’ble Supreme Court Committee on Road Safety .

10. Transport Commisioner, Government of Maharashtra : Reducing Road fatalities on Indian roads by 50% as per Brasilia Declaration and guidelines laid down by Hon’ble Supreme Court Committee on Road Safety in six districts with high fatality rates in Maharashtra viz. Pune, Thane, Ahmednagar, Solapur, Dhule & Palghar.
