WHAT WE DO

Our Works

  • Awareness generation for Disaster Preparedness.
  • Awareness campaign and providing guidance on Preparedness of the community and all the stakeholders against the following Disasters:
    1. Geological related Disasters.
    2. Medical Related Disasters.
    3. Terrorist related Disasters.
    4. Water and climatic Related disasters with special emphasis to cyclones, drought, famines, floods and drainage problems, cloud burst, heat/cold waves, sea coast erosion, thunder and lightning strike and Tsunami.
    5. Biological related Disasters on cattle epidemics, food poisoning and pest attacks.
    6. Chemical, environmental, industrial related Disasters. Disaster related to pollution, deforestation and wild life.
    7. Accident related disasters include structural collapses, explosion, urban and village fires, electrical fires, festival related accidents and rail/road accidents.
  • Education and Training on Disaster Management
  • Mitigation and Contingency Planning
  • Training for Govt Schools & Private Schools regarding Disaster Management.
  • Training for Govt Officers, Police Officers & Police Personnel on Disaster Management.
  • Crime Prevention
  • Formation of village level Task Force
  • Formation of Disaster Management Committees and Teams
  • Development of Disaster Management Plans and Conduct of mock drills
  • Crowd Management and Traffic Management in mass scale gatherings and public events
  • Vulnerability assessment and coordination with Government and Non Government agencies
  • Hazard Mapping
  • Comprehensive Disaster Risk Management Framework
  • Financial Strategies for Managing Economic Impacts of Disasters
  • Earthquake Risk Reduction
  • Drought mitigation strategies
  • Gender aspects of Disaster Recovery and Reconstruction
  • Risk Analysis
  • Risk Sensitive Land Use Planning
  • Damage and Reconstruction Needs Assessment
  • Community based Disaster Risk Management
  • Community induced early warnings and preparedness
  • Safe Cities
  • Climate Change and Disaster Risk
  • Urban Resilience and Sustainable Land use Planning.
  • Water Resources Management
  • Environmental Law and Policy
  • Conducting activities and imparting training in all the current and future sectors in which NSDC (National Skill Development Corporation) and SSDC (State Skill Development Corporation) provides services for.
